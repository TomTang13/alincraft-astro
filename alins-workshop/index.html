Our Story

这里是阿琳手作，一个始于"慢"，忠于"专注"的地方。

主理人阿琳，是一个在潮汕老家"静"着长大的姑娘。在她的记忆里，手作不是用来被浪漫化的消遣，而是一种日复一日的修行。

她的祖父今年八十高龄，依然坚持着潮汕珠绣的创作。细若游丝的针、流光溢彩的绸缎、米粒大小的玻璃珠……在那漫长的时光里，阿琳看着祖父重复着同一个动作，没有催促，只有呼吸与手势的同频。

正是那份沉默的笃定，让她早早便懂得：手作，不仅是技巧的堆叠，更是一种让心安住的方式。

带着这份家传的静气，阿琳遇见了编织。相比珠绣的严谨，毛线是柔软的，它允许人按照自己的步调呼吸。

如今，这间工作室落地上海。在这座讲究效率的城市里，阿琳想提供一个"反效率"的角落。

真正的温暖，从来不是做得多快，而是愿意陪你多久。

Welcome to Alin Handcraft, a place born from "slowness" and dedicated to "focus".

A-Lin, the creator behind this studio, grew up quietly in her hometown in Chaoshan. In her memory, handcraft is not a romanticized pastime, but a daily practice.

Her grandfather, now in his eighties, still persists in Chaoshan bead embroidery. The fine needle, the lustrous silk, the glass beads the size of rice grains... In those long years, A-Lin watched her grandfather repeat the same motion, without rush, only breath and gesture in rhythm.

It was that silent certainty that taught her early on: handcraft is not just a stack of techniques, but a way to let the heart rest.

Carrying this inherited stillness, A-Lin found her way to knitting. Compared to the precision of bead embroidery, yarn is soft; it allows one to breathe at their own pace.

Today, this studio has landed in Shanghai. In this city that values efficiency, A-Lin wants to offer a corner of "counter-efficiency".

True warmth is not about how fast you create, but how long you are willing to stay.

关于阿琳

About A-Lin

在南京生活的数年间，阿琳将这份耐心化作了指尖的肌肉记忆。从基础针法到复杂结构，她用时间打磨手感，也在一针一线中完成了自我对话。

在课程中，我们不赶进度。针法可以慢慢学，作品不必急于一时。比起结果，阿琳更在意你是否在重复的动作中，卸下了紧绷的情绪。

在定制中，我们不走流水线。每一件玩偶都由阿琳亲手制作。因为她相信，只有带着体温的手指，才能将那些具体而真实的情感，缝进作品的灵魂里。

During her years living in Nanjing, A-Lin transformed this patience into muscle memory in her fingertips. From basic stitches to complex structures, she honed her craft with time, completing a dialogue with herself in every stitch.

In our classes, we don't rush. Stitches can be learned slowly, and there's no need to hurry to finish a piece. More than the result, A-Lin cares about whether you have released your tension in the repetitive motions.

In custom orders, we don't follow the assembly line. Every doll is handcrafted by A-Lin herself. Because she believes only fingers with warmth can sew real, tangible emotions into the soul of a creation.
